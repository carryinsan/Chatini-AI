The Architecture of Trust

We inverted the standard AI model. Your data is yours. We process logic, but we do not harvest your life. Here is how we guarantee your privacy and output validity.

Local-First Storage

There are no remote databases holding your files hostage. Your chat history, Google Drive links, and uploaded documents remain strictly inside your browser's IndexedDB. Close the tab, and your environment is secure.

No Black Boxes

Every math graph, study deck, or web app generated by LexisAI is fully transparent. You can inspect the structural logic, copy the raw JSON/HTML, and export your artifacts freely without vendor lock-in.

Zero Hallucinations

Our backend utilizes aggressive multi-key rotation and structural edge sanitizers. If the AI generates invalid data, LexisAI detects it instantly and throws a secure UI alert—never feeding you false logic.

Operational Guide

Master the LexisAI interface in 4 simple steps.

1

Inject Context

Open the Workspace. Click the Paperclip to upload PDFs, Markdown, or Code. Alternatively, click the Google Drive icon to authenticate and sync cloud documents locally.

2

Arm the Engine

Above the chat bar, select your operational mode. Toggle Research for reports, Slides for PPTX, Maths for graphing, or App for web tools.

3

Execute Prompt

Type your command and hit Enter. LexisAI will map the neural pathways, compile the logic, and stream the generated Artifact directly into your right-side visual canvas.

4

Export & Deploy

Inside the Artifact Workspace, click Download to save native PDF or PPTX files to your machine, or click Publish to generate a secure, shareable web link.
